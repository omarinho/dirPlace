* Who is dirPlace for?

There are two types of users:

1. People who are looking for discounts, coupons or offers online.
2. Owners/managers of commercial places who want to promote their businesses online and build a community of customers on Internet.

* What is dirPlace?

It's an online platform to look for discounts, coupons and offers. People can get promotions by becoming fan of places that are offering the deals. Business owners/managers can promote their places by using some tools. Users can find the deals by searching by keyword or browsing by category.

Go up

* When should dirPlace be used?

Whenever you want to find out/get discounts, coupons or offers for products and services. If you are a place owner/manager, you should use this platform if you want to build an online community of fans/customers by offering some promotions that allow your business fans/customers to save money.

Go up

* Where is dirPlace available?

Anywhere where there is an Internet connection.

Go up

* Why should I use dirPlace?

Because you can find promotions you really need. Not only "the deal of the day". And, if you are a place owner/manager, because you can promote and market your business with a high ROI (for free for the first 1,000 fans).

Go up

* How does dirPlace work?

For you:

1. Search for discounts, coupons and offers, by keyword or category.
2. You will get a list of current promotions within your specified reach. Even you can use GPS-enabled devices to see a list of places close to you.
3. If you have an account and becomes a fan of the place offering the promotion, you can get the benefit.

For your business:

1. Register your business.
2. Create a promotion for our users: A discount, a coupon or an offer.
3. That's all. Since the promotions only apply for your fans, you'll be ready to build an online community of loyal customers. We will drive traffic to your business' profile for free. You'll be able to invite your current customers too.

You don't need to register (or create an account) to use our search engine to find out promotions. Also, you can browse the promotions by category without an account. But you need an account to become fan of a place or register a business.

A promotion is any type of deal you can find in this platform: A discount, a coupon or an offer.

* How much it cost to use dirPlace for getting promotions?

Nothing. Zero. It's free.

* When can I get the promotions?

There is no a general restriction about it. However, a place owner/manager can restrict promotions in a range of dates, and of course, you need to take into account the open hours of the place.

* A place is lying about its promotions. Can I report it?

Yes. You can report it by sending us a message to misuse@dirplace.com
You also can rate the place and write a review at the profile of that place to alert other users.

Go up

* Can I get all the promotions I want?

Yes. However, keep in mind that a place owner/manager can restrict a promotion to be available only for people who have been fans during, at least, the last three months or the last twelve months.

* How can I identify myself as a fan of a place?

Each user has a chosen or automatically assigned ID. You can print a physical card with your picture and your ID; the card is available at the My Account section. Alternatively, in your profile, we display a QR Code codified with your ID which you can show at the place as a way to identify yourself. At the place, your ID can be verified in our system as a fan of them.

We strongly advise you to print (and maybe laminate) the card as a way to identify yourself at the places.

* Is dirplace a marketing tool?

If you are an owner or manager of a commercial place opened to the public, the answer is: Yes! You can use this platform to promote your business and, at the same time, you will be building a community of customers online.

  • US$ 9 per month from 1,001 to 10,000 fans.
  • US$ 99 per month from 10,001 to 100,000 fans.
  • US$ 999 per month from 100,001 to 1,000,000 fans.
  • And so on.

    * What type of promotions can I create for my business?

    There are three types of promotions:

    1. Discounts: Your fans can get a discount percent when buying your products or when getting your services. Example: Get 20% off on all sandwiches.

    2. Coupons: Your fans have a coupon for a specified amount to be redeemed at your place. Example: Coupon valid for $30 off in-store.

    3. Offers: Your fans can get an additional benefit when buying your products or when they get your services. Example: Buy 1 get 1 free.

    Go up

    * Can I use dirPlace for several businesses of mine?

    Sure. There is no restriction at all.

    Go up

    * Can I limit the promotions only for some products/services?

    Yes. When you create a promotion, there is a field called Restrictions. There, you can explicitly describe what products/services can apply for that promotion.

    Go up

    * Can I limit the promotions only for some fans?

    Yes. You can restrict the promotions to be applicable for:

    1. All fans (default)
    2. People who have been fans for, at least, the last three months.
    3. People who have been fans for, at least, the last twelve months.

    Go up

    * How can I identify the fans when they come to my place?

    You need an Internet connection to verify your place's fans.* Each user of this platform has a numerical ID. With this ID, your fans can identify themselves by showing either a physical card we provide them, or showing a QR Code. You can ask for their numerical ID (verbally or printed) and verify if the person is a fan by entering the ID at the My Place Profile section. In that section, we provide you a tool for that purpose (there, you also can see the date when the person became a fan and her/his name).

    If a fan doesn't have the physical card and he only provides the ID verbally, you should ask for an alternative document (identity card, driving license, social security card) in order to avoid deceit. That way, you can check if fan's name in our system matches the name in the document.

    IMPORTANT: Please, keep in mind that not all users with an ID or card are necessarily fans of your place. Ultimately, the verification of fans identity it's up to you. Just keep it simple.

    * If you don't have an Internet connection at your place, you could keep a periodically updated printed list of fans to verify them manually (we provide you a tool to print a fans list at the My Place Profile section). However, that approach is not practical in all cases (think about people who become fans since a few minutes ago: They won't be in your printed list). In that case, keep in mind that you also can use a basic smartphone or a small tablet with a mobile internet connection.

    Go up

    * I just registered my business. Can I invite my current customers?

    Sure. The public profile of your place in our website has an easy and optimized URL. For example: http://dirplace.com/New-York/MY-PLACE-NAME/

    You can invite your current customers; they can visit your place's profile and they can become fans. In addition, at the My Place Profile section we provide you with share buttons to invite current followers or fans of your place at other social networks like Facebook, Twitter or Instagram.

    Lastly, but not least, you can embed a widget (provided by us) in your website to encourage your web visitors to become fans.

    Go up

    * How can I promote my profile?

    It's up to you. We suggest three ways:

    - By promoting the URL of your place's profile both offline and online. You can use paid advertising, of course. And there are plenty of ways to promote it online for free.

    - By inviting your fans or followers in other websites like Facebook, Twitter or Instagram. We provide you some buttons at the My Place Profile section to do that.

    - By linking your place's profile from your website. We provide you a widget for that at the My Place Profile section.
